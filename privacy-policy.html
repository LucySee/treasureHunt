🏴‍☠️ Privacy Policy – Treasure Hunt

Last updated: March 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Lucy See ("I", "me", or "my") handles information in the Treasure Hunt app ("the App"). I am committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information I Collect

The Treasure Hunt app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to any server or third party. All data you enter into the app (hunt names, clues, secret codes, and drawings) is stored locally on your device only using your browser's local storage.

2. Data Storage

All app data is saved directly on your device. I do not have access to any of the content you create in the app. If you uninstall the app or clear your browser data, all stored data will be permanently deleted.

3. Children's Privacy

The Treasure Hunt app is designed for use by families and children. The app does not collect any personal information from children or adults. No account registration is required. No data leaves your device.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses Google Fonts (Fredoka One, Nunito, Black Han Sans) for display purposes. Google Fonts may log standard web request information such as your IP address when fonts are loaded. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

5. Permissions

The app does not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, location, or any other device permissions.

6. Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
